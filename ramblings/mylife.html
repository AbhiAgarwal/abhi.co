my life

My life has been a picnic compared to lives of others. I've never been discriminated against. In any way. I was brought up in a safe environment at a safe high-school with an incredible family and supportive friends. I grew up in Bangkok.

Growing up in the US is said to be so incredible outside the US, but as I've read more and more about issues that occur in the US it seems crazy and quite a shitshow if you're not the average white kid.
